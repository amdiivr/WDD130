The New Lightspeed Wireless RGB Logitech for gaming.

The Logitech G915 TKL is a full-size mechanical keyboard of the TKL type, that is, with the number part removed. Its width is less than 37 mm, and all this without giving up enough function keys or fabulous performance and comfort of use. The materials and manufacturing details are appreciated as soon as you put your fingers on it. The body is made of high-quality and robust plastic, but it is also finished off with an aluminum alloy top plate that gives it a lot of presence and a steel-reinforced base. With all this we achieve a very interesting balance between lightness (810 grams without cable) and thickness (22 mm) without giving up enormous robustness and stability on the table. And a lot of comfort of use due to its low profile. The result is excellent in practically all its facets. Finish, quality of the switches, reliability of the connectivity, autonomy and benefits are at the top of the peripheral market of this cut. So, if you make the investment, you will not be disappointed.

Purpose

The purpose of the MARAND site is to be able to share information about the technological world, gadgets, stories and news about technological progress, in addition we will be able to see some comments from our critics regarding products that we have tested so that the public can have an idea of how to apply it in your life and if you want in the end to buy it for a useful use. this is MARAND.

Audience

For those who like technology, the use of them and how to know if it really works correctly, here you can see how these news and innovations can be a complement in our lives and how we can use it properly.

Microsoft Teams would implement the metaverse with avatars within meetings. Microsoft has announced its new modality to enter meetings through Teams with Mesh, it will be a platform with the aim of obtaining virtual experiences by adding its 2020 Together Mode tool, which helps to be closer to the participants in scheduled meetings. The avatars that you can create would be in 2D or 3D and it would help to have an approach to the metaverse environment, you can create your avatar and enter the meeting or simply turn on your webcam and participate without the need to connect to VR glasses, the experience of participant creates a virtual social environment to which Facebook tries to demonstrate what it would do in Meta. Mesh for Teams would arrive in mid-2022 and will also have spaces where you can introduce yourself and interact much more with your avatar using Microsoft AI to hear your voice, use applications and even play games.

Corsair's new Voyager a1600 would be the company's first laptop with a Touchbar. The new laptop is powered by Ryzen 6000 series and AMD Radeon RX 6000 series processors, making it Corsair's first laptop made for gaming. What characterizes much more about the new laptop is the way that the TouchBar can be used, this new implementation for them they call direct access for the "S" key, although the name is not short, it refers to the utility that you can have with the touch bar, according to Corsair it is not a bar where other keys are replaced but it is personalized because its usefulness would be compared to the Elgato's stream deck, implying that it would help a lot in the streaming service. Physically the device was shown during AMD's Computex keynote and according to the renders they show us what the final product would look like. According to the technical specifications you could choose between the following: Ryzen 7 6800HS Ryzen 9 6900HS Both configurations come with a Radeon 6800M GPU. You can get up to 64GB of RAM (Corsair Vengeance DDR5) and 2TB of storage. It would have a 16-inch 2560 x 1600, 240 Hz screen and two Thunderbolt 3 USB 4.0 ports, one USB 3.2 Gen 2 Type C, one USB 3.2 Gen 1 Type A, an SDXC 7.0 card reader and an audio jack, FHD 1080p camera and full-size Cherry MX low-profile mechanical keyboard with RGB backlighting.

Tesla owners will soon be able to access digitally distributed games inside their cars. Although decent free games are already present in the car since 2019, such as “Solitaire and chess”, oldies arcade “Missile command and Super Breakcout” and other classics, now Tesla is committed to integrating the Steam digital gaming platform for next month according to Elon Musk's response to a video on Twitter. The arrival of Steam has great potential to take the gaming experience inside the car to another level, because we would have more than 50,000 games, playing from Counter-Strike to Elder Ring. Currently the Model S and Model X runs on an AMD Ryzen CPU and AMD RDNA-2 GPU until it can run the game Cyberpunk 2077 according to a Twitter reference by Elon Musk as well. What is not allowed is to play it during autopilot because this led to an investigation in 2021 by the NHTSA in the United States.

The New Lightspeed Wireless RGB Logitech for gaming. The Logitech G915 TKL is a full-size mechanical keyboard of the TKL type, that is, with the number part removed. Its width is less than 37 mm, and all this without giving up enough function keys or fabulous performance and comfort of use. The materials and manufacturing details are appreciated as soon as you put your fingers on it. The body is made of high-quality and robust plastic, but it is also finished off with an aluminum alloy top plate that gives it a lot of presence and a steel-reinforced base. With all this we achieve a very interesting balance between lightness (810 grams without cable) and thickness (22 mm) without giving up enormous robustness and stability on the table. And a lot of comfort of use due to its low profile. The result is excellent in practically all its facets. Finish, quality of the switches, reliability of the connectivity, autonomy and benefits are at the top of the peripheral market of this cut. So, if you make the investment, you will not be disappointed.

Microsoft Teams would implement the metaverse with avatars within meetings

Microsoft has announced its new modality to enter meetings through Teams with Mesh, it will be a platform with the aim of obtaining virtual experiences by adding its 2020 Together Mode tool, which helps to be closer to the participants in scheduled meetings. The avatars that you can create would be in 2D or 3D and it would help to have an approach to the metaverse environment, you can create your avatar and enter the meeting or simply turn on your webcam and participate without the need to connect to VR glasses, the experience of participant creates a virtual social environment to which Facebook tries to demonstrate what it would do in Meta. Mesh for Teams would arrive in mid-2022 and will also have spaces where you can introduce yourself and interact much more with your avatar using Microsoft AI to hear your voice, use applications and even play games.
